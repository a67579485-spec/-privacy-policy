Privacy Policy

Extension: ChatGPT Prompt Generator  |  Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the ChatGPT Prompt Generator browser extension ("the Extension", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. The Extension helps automate prompt generation on ChatGPT directly in your browser, without relying on any external server.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of the following:

  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history
  • Passwords or account credentials
  • Financial or payment information
  • Any data sent to external servers or third parties

The Extension does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising SDKs of any kind.

2. Information Stored Locally

The Extension uses your browser's built-in chrome.storage.local API to save the following data only on your own device:

  • Your prompt list / queue and generation settings
  • App state (e.g. progress, pause/resume status)
  • Local activity logs used for error recovery and debugging

This data never leaves your device, is never sent to us or any third party, and is automatically removed if you uninstall the Extension.

3. Downloads Permission

The Extension may use the browser's downloads permission solely to let you save generated results (e.g. as a text or CSV file) directly to your computer, at your own request. We do not access or read any of your other downloaded files.

4. Host Permissions

The Extension only runs on chatgpt.com and chat.openai.com to read and automate prompt input/output on those pages. It does not run on, monitor, or collect data from any other website you visit.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any user data with third parties, because the Extension does not collect or transmit any data off of your device in the first place.

6. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
